ClearName Guide
HOW TO USE THIS TOOL
What is ClearName?

ClearName matches missing persons reports against unidentified remains cases from NamUs, surfacing potential connections missed due to jurisdictional gaps.

⚠ All matches are algorithmic candidates only — not confirmed identifications. Every match must be verified by a trained investigator before any action is taken.

Do I need to download anything?

If someone shared a link with you: No download needed. Open the link and data is already loaded.

If you have the files locally: Click Load HTML Reports in the sidebar and select the match_report_XX.html files from your reports/ folder.

How to review a match
1
Open both NamUs case records.

Click View NamUs Case on both sides. Compare physical descriptions, tattoos, scars, clothing, and circumstances.

2
Check Reasons to Doubt.

If the body was found before the disappearance date, verify dates on NamUs before proceeding.

3
Check NamUs for existing matches or exclusions.

If this pair already appears on NamUs, do not resubmit it.

4
Select a status and save.

Click Review on the card. Choose Verified, Excluded, or Unsure. Add notes and click Save Review.

5
Submit verified matches to NamUs.

Click Submit to NamUs Case Officer to open a pre-filled email with all match details ready to send.
